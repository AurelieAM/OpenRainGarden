Learn More

Hui o Ko'olaupoko (HOK) is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, established in 2007, to work with communities to imporove water quality through ecosystem restoration and non-point source (NPS) pollution reduction. HOK applies innovative strategies to restoration, monitoring, best management practices and education/outreach programs. HOK's mission is to "protect ocean health by restorting the aina: mauka to makai."

HOK is a leader in Storm Water Best Management Practices in Hawai'i. Please visit HOK's website to learn more about how you can reduce the impacts of storm water pollution and help protect our oceans! www.huihawaii.org
